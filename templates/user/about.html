Who Are We?

Welcome to Healthy Sabji! We are here to bring you fresh and healthy fruits and vegetables straight from local farmers.

Our goal is to connect farmers with people in cities, making it easier for everyone to enjoy fresh food and support local farming. In Nepal, a lot of fruits and vegetables go to waste because there are not enough storage places, and transportation is not well-organized. People in cities find it hard to get fresh, good-quality produce. At the same time, farmers face difficulties selling their products and earning fair profits because middlemen take a large share.

Healthy Sabji was created to fix these problems. We help farmers sell directly to customers, reducing waste and making sure everyone gets fresh, affordable produce.
